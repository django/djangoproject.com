Django's Ecosystem

Django's Secret Sauce is its ecosystem of third-party apps and add-ons.

No matter your need, there'll be a package you can use or take ideas from.

Resources

  • Awesome Django > A curated list of awesome things related to Django. https://awesomedjango.org.
  • Django Packages >a directory of reusable apps, sites, tools, and more for your Django projects. https://djangopackages.org
  • Jazzband > a collaborative community to share the responsibility of maintaining Python-based (but mainly Django) projects. https://jazzband.co/projects
  • Django Commons > an organization dedicated to supporting the community's efforts to maintain packages. https://github.com/django-commons
  • Django News > Weekly Django news, articles, projects, and more. https://django-news.com
  • DjangoChat Podcast > The OP podcast on the Django Web Framework https://djangochat.com
  • DjangoBrew Podcast > A fun, caffeine-powered podcast about the Django web https://djangobrew.com
  • DjangoGirls Podcast > A podcast sharing the stories of attendees, coaches and organisers of DjangoGirls workshops around the world. https://creators.spotify.com/pod/show/djangogirls
  • DjangoBook.com > An aggregator for in-print books and courses on Django, sorted by most recent Django version updated. https://djangobook.com
  • DCUS YouTube https://www.youtube.com/@DjangoConUS
  • DCEU You Tube https://www.youtube.com/@DjangoConEurope
  • Django.tv https://djangotv.com
